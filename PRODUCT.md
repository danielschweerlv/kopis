# PRODUCT.md

## Product

KOPIS War Room is a founder command center for validating, packaging, and operating Kopis: a payroll linked repayment system.

## Register

product

## Users

- Founder and operator preparing the next 7 to 30 days of work.
- Counsel reviewing state, servicing, assignment, consent, and middleware questions.
- Pilot lenders evaluating the repayment rail and operational lift.
- Investors evaluating the market wedge, moat, and execution sequence.

## Positioning

Kopis is a payroll linked repayment system. Third-party lenders integrate once, then orchestrate borrower-consented repayment across payroll connectivity providers with compliance controls, servicing, reconciliation, exception handling, and job-change continuity.

## Non-Claims

- Kopis is not a lender.
- Kopis is not a payroll API.
- Kopis did not invent payroll deduction.
- "Plaid for payroll" is not the primary frame.
- "Financial wellness" is not the buyer-facing pitch.

## Product Purpose

Convert research into an operating surface: executive status, legal matrix, competitor matrix, pilot tracker, MVP scope, fundraising checklist, audience packets, and borrower trust draft.

## Tone

Sharp, institutional, direct, and evidence-labeled. Write for lender diligence and legal review, not hype.

## Current Risks

- Voluntary wage assignment and revocation rules need counsel review in pilot states.
- Money transmitter and servicer role analysis is unresolved.
- Payroll middleware terms must allow repayment use cases.
- The first vertical should stay personal or medical installment lending, not mortgage.
