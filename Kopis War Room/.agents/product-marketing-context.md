# Kopis — Product & Marketing Context

## What Kopis Is

**Lender-agnostic payroll repayment infrastructure.** Kopis sits between payroll providers and lenders as an orchestration layer — routing repayments, enforcing rules, and handling continuity when employment changes.

The gap it fills: no one has built a clean orchestration layer connecting payroll systems to lending repayment at scale. The market assumes lenders build point-to-point integrations. Kopis replaces that with a shared infrastructure rail.

## Audience

- **Primary**: Lenders (community banks, credit unions, fintechs) wanting payroll-linked repayment without building it in-house
- **Secondary**: Employers and HR/payroll platforms wanting to offer embedded lending benefits
- **Tertiary**: Investors evaluating the infrastructure + platform thesis
