## Design Context

### Users
Joshua Walters (founder) and counsel review this on a desktop monitor during diligence and weekly operating sessions. Occasional investor viewing. Long sessions, dense data, needs to be calm and readable for extended focus work.

### Brand Personality
Bank infrastructure + legal operating room + lender diligence portal.
**3 words**: institutional, operational, precise.
Not a consumer product. Not a startup landing page. Not an AI dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark graphite command center. Kopis signal green appears only for active/ready state — it is NOT a dominant color. Multi-accent route system (blue, iris, violet, pink, coral, amber) used for section navigation identity only.

Anti-references: Generic AI dark-green glow. Purple/blue SaaS gradients. Decorative blobs or glassmorphism. Fake metrics or sparklines. Rainbow card decks.

Theme: Night (dark) primary, Day (light) toggle available.

### Typography
Display font for h1: **Barlow Semi Condensed** (600/700) — condensed grotesque, authoritative, institutional weight.
Body/UI font: **Karla** (variable, 400–800) — clean humanist grotesque, excellent at small sizes (0.82–0.9rem), good for dense data tables.
Neither font is in the standard AI-default list.

### Design Principles
1. Readability over decoration — every element earns its visual weight
2. Signal discipline — accent color only where it indicates state or section identity
3. Institutional density — packed but scannable, not sparse or decorative
4. No invented confidence — legal/compliance claims stay labeled as provisional
5. Panels breathe — consistent but not monotonous rhythm between sections
