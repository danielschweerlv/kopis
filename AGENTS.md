# AGENTS.md

KOPIS local guidance for Codex sessions.

## Project

- KOPIS is a payroll linked repayment system.
- It is not a lender, not a payroll API, and should not claim it invented payroll deduction.
- The product is a neutral orchestration, compliance, servicing, reconciliation, borrower consent, and job-change continuity layer for third-party lenders.

## Startup

- Read `PRODUCT.md`, `DESIGN.md`, and `docs/agent/kopis-war-room-handoff.md` before major edits.
- Preserve local screenshots and handoff docs.
- This folder may not be a git repo. Check before running git commands.

## Design Direction

- Treat the app as an institutional founder command center: bank infrastructure, legal operating room, lender diligence portal.
- Use Kopis green as signal, not glow.
- Prefer dense, scannable operational surfaces over marketing sections.
- Label evidence clearly: `Verified`, `Likely`, `Needs counsel`, `Assumption`.

## Legal Guardrails

- Do not state legal conclusions as final.
- Mark voluntary wage assignment, money transmitter, servicer role, middleware terms, and pilot-state scope as counsel-dependent until verified.
