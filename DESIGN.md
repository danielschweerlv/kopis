# DESIGN.md

## Visual System

KOPIS War Room should feel like bank infrastructure plus legal operating room plus lender diligence portal.

## Theme

Founder and counsel review this on a desktop monitor during diligence and weekly operating sessions. The surface should be calm, dark graphite, and readable for long sessions. Kopis green appears only as a signal color.

## Color

- Base: warm graphite and ink neutrals.
- Panel: slightly lifted graphite surfaces with low-contrast borders.
- Accent: Kopis signal green for active state, readiness, and positive deltas.
- Risk: amber for assumptions and counsel gates.
- Critical: red only for blocking legal or operational risk.
- Info: muted blue for neutral diligence and source notes.

## Typography

- Use system UI fonts.
- Keep headings compact and functional.
- Body copy should be concise, 65 to 75 characters per line in prose areas.
- Data labels and tables should stay dense and scan-friendly.

## Layout

- App shell with left navigation, top status bar, and main operating surface.
- No decorative card stacks. Use panels, tables, matrices, rails, and checklists.
- Desktop should prioritize side-by-side matrices.
- Mobile should collapse navigation into horizontal tabs and stack panels.

## Component Rules

- Evidence chips: Verified, Likely, Needs counsel, Assumption.
- Status chips: Ready, In review, Blocked, Draft, Next.
- Tables need clear headers, compact rows, and no fake precision.
- Buttons use icons where useful and maintain visible focus states.

## Bans

- Generic dark-green AI dashboard glow.
- Purple or blue SaaS gradients.
- "Plaid for payroll" as hero language.
- Decorative blobs, glass panels, stock avatars, fake charts, and nested cards.
- Broad legal certainty without counsel labels.
